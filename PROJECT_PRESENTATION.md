## 2. Problem Statement (Samasya Kya Hai?)
Traditonal medical stores mein kuch common problems hoti hain:
1.  **Stockouts:** Pata hi nahi chalta kab dawai khatam ho gayi. Customer wapas chala jata hai.
2.  **Expiry Issues:** Dawai expire ho jati hai aur nuksan hota hai.
3.  **Human Error:** Billing karte waqt galti ho sakti hai.
4.  **Safety Risks:** Kabhi-kabhi pharmacist galti se do aisi dawai de deta hai jo sath mein nahi leni chahiye (Drug Interaction).

---

## 3. Our Solution (Humara Samadhan)
Mera project in problems ko solve karta hai:
- **Intelligent Inventory:** Stock kam hone par automatic alert deta hai.
- **AI Predictions:** Past sales data ko analyze karke batata hai ki agli dawai kab mangwani padegi.
- **Safety Checks:** Agar bill mein dangerous combination add kiya, to turant warning milti hai.

---

## 4. Key Features (Mukhya Khasiyat)

### A. AI Stock Prediction (Linear Regression)
Human guess karne ke bajaye, humara system **Machine Learning** use karta hai.
- **Kaise kaam karta hai:** Ye pichle mahino ki sales dekhta hai -> Trend analyze karta hai -> Aur predict karta hai ki current stock kitne din chalega.
- **Benefit:** "Zero Stockouts, Zero Overstocking."

### B. Drug Interaction Checker (Safety First)
- Agar patient 'Aspirin' le raha hai, aur pharmacist galti se 'Warfarin' add kar de, to system **POP-UP ALERT** deta hai: *"Critical Risk: Bleeding Incident possible."*
- Ye feature patients ki jaan bacha sakta hai.

### C. Voice Search
- Pharmacist ko type karne ki zarurat nahi. Bas mic click karo aur bolo *"Paracetamol"* — system dawai dhund lega.

### D. Smart Billing & Analytics
- Fast billing system jo apne aap stock adjust karta hai.
- **Python-powered Dashboard** jo charts ke through batata hai ki konsi dawai sabse zyada bik rahi hai.
